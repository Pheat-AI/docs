## Edit

Use natural language to edit any aspect of the image using the prompt box below the image. This powerful feature lets you make complex edits by simply describing what you want to change.

<Tip>

  images that guide your edits. Reference images help the AI better understand
  your desired changes and produce more accurate results. Ideal for adding
  products or characters on a scene.
</Tip>
