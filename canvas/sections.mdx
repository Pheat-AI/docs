## Creating a Section

To create a new section on your canvas:

1. **Access the sections tool** - Click on the sections icon in the side toolbar
2. **Draw the section area** - Click and drag to draw the area where you want the section to be added
3. **Organize your content** - Add and remove items to the section by placing them inside it or moving them outside
